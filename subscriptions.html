Common Questions

Can I skip a delivery?

Yes! You can pause or skip deliveries up to 24 hours before your scheduled date via your account page.

Are the flowers pet safe?

We offer a specific "Pet Friendly" preference in your account settings that ensures no lilies or toxic plants are included.

Subscription deliveries go out on Wednesdays and Fridays to ensure you have fresh blooms for the weekend.
